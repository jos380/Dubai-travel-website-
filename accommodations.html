Where to Stay

Curated picks for different traveller styles — luxury, family, beachfront and budget.

Burj Al Arab — Ultimate Luxury

Iconic sail-shaped hotel on its own island — private beaches and Michelin-star dining.

  • Best for special occasions
  • Tip: Book dining + suite packages

Atlantis, The Palm — Family & Fun

Aquaventure waterpark, aquarium and family-friendly facilities on Palm Jumeirah.

  • Best for families
  • Tip: Book ocean-view rooms for aquarium views

Rove Hotels — Smart Budget

Stylish budget chain with modern rooms and great locations for city access.

  • Best for: Budget travellers
